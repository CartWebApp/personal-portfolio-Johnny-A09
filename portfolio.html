Project: "BioShield Insect Repellant"

[Project Overview]

BioShield is a fake company that a multi-page site was created for, with the purpose of teaching the creators basic communication and collaboration skills, since it was done in pairs, as well as being an introduction to basic HTML and web development as a whole.

1. The Technology Stack

  • Languages: HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript.
  • Tools: Visual Studio Code, and ChatGPT for the development of the site.
  • Deployment: Hosted through Netlify as a free and secure way to deploy the site.

2. Concepts Learned

  • Semantic HTML: Learned the basics of HTML5, and how it was to be organized in a basic site.
  • Collaboration: Worked in a pair for the duration of the site's creation, teaching how to work with someone that I had never interacted with.
  • Communication: Taught the importance of communication when working with someone on a project.

3. Challenges & Solutions

  • The Problem: I had trouble making my styling neat and organized in the file making it hard to find what I was looking for at times.
    The Solution: I had no solution at the time it was finished, but to fix it I would go back and organize the styling in a simple and comprehensive way.
  • The Problem: Going into the project I had little to no knowledge or experience in web development.
    The Solution: I took it as I went and did what I could trying and failing until something worked.
  • The Problem: The images given by the AI used for them were undesirable and didn't look good to us.
    The Solution: I changed what AI I used for the images as well as changed the prompts that I gave to receive images that I was satisfied with.

4. Habits of Mind in Action (at least 2)

  • Investigate: When running into problems I had to look everywhere to find where the source of said problem was.
  • Initiate: To actually start learning and begin the process of trial and error throughout the project I had to actually start testing things to find a solution.

Project: "PawPal Pet Wellness Tracker"

[Project Overview]

Again a fake company the site was created for, working in pairs as well. The project was meant to teach CSS, with the main component being media queries/screen size responsiveness.

1. The Technology Stack

  • Languages: HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript.
  • Tools: Visual Studio Code, and ChatGPT for the development of the site.
  • Deployment: Hosted through Netlify as a free and secure way to deploy the site.

2. Concepts Learned

  • AI Ethics: I learned how to utilize AI ethically without using it to do the work for me but instead using it as an assistant helping me through the troubles.
  • CSS3: I learned how to put the concepts previously learned about CSS into practice making the site look exactly how it was envisioned.
  • Screen Responsiveness: I learned how to adjust how the pages of the site looked through CSS media queries to ensure the site would look proper throughout all devices.

3. Challenges & Solutions

  • The Problem: I was creating the screen size responsiveness, which went smoothly apart from the navbar which once hosted on netlify, had broken and the images used for said navbar would not show up.
    The Solution: The issue wasn't able to be fixed, so instead I changed the buttons from images to text as a quick change, since all else failed.
  • The Problem: I had issues coming with design choices for the site including how we wanted the style of the site to be.
    The Solution: I collaborated with my partner and we discussed the matter, eventually coming up with how we wanted the site to look.
  • The Problem: I had problems with deadlines and meeting them properly.
    The Solution: Each deadline was met on time, but without its stresses. To achieve the deadlines I had to talk with my partner on what to do and complete as much as I could in the timeframe.

4. Habits of Mind in Action (at least 2)

  • Communicate: I had to communicate with my partner from home to accomplish what we wanted without issues or conflicts.
  • Collaborate: I worked with my partner well to ensure everything was smooth and efficient.
